about walk my shoes

What you'll find here are all my own words, thoughts and feelings. It's raw, personal, vulnerable and absolutely terrifying to share, but I have chosen to do it. To unburden myself of years of carrying the weight of my emotions, but also with the hope that some of it will resonate with others and encourage deeper connections. Because if I have learned something in the past years of my own search is that some emotions, situations, responses are universal; That we all have our own world of trauma and drama and pain and joy and we are all looking for a way to make it work.

Most of the essays are still draft versions (if I aimed for perfection I would never share) and the blog posts are written on the spot or edited versions of notes I keep on my phone.

I feel uncomfortable calling myself a writer, but I write, because I do believe I have something to say and a message that can find common ground in other people. I'm still finding my voice but I want you to be part of it. The words and the process.
